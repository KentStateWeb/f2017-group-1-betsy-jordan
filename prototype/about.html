Our Story

For over ten years we have sourced, roasted, blended and served our award winning coffee to Ohio. We offer a variety of estate and co-op coffees as well as a few select and specialized blends. We value the importance of fairness and quality at each step of the process from seed to cup. We also offer a selection of coffee-related supplies and equipment that we endorse. The Art of Coffee

Our batch size (20-30 lbs. per batch) is small enough to give us good control and flexibility with our roast profile, and large enough to give a good amount of bean-to-bean radiant heat transfer and roast evenness. We exclusively air-cool our beans because water-quenching degrades flavor intensity and clarity. Roasting coffee to perfection is exciting, but not mysterious. Hereâ€™s how we do it:

  • Roast with artistry and advanced technology.
  • Control quality from grower to roaster to barista to consumer.

Smaller is Better

Many of our specialty beans come from a single farm or even from a specific area on the farm, so they are limited in quantity and superior in quality. We buy directly from the farmers and have established strong, long-term relationships, based on good coffee and fair prices.
